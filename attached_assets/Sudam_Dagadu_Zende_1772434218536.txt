Sudam Dagadu Zende: Champion of Banking, Rural Empowerment, and Social Change
Sudam Dagadu Zende is a distinguished banking professional and social leader. An M.Sc. and LL.B. graduate, he served as Chief Manager at State Bank of India. He has empowered rural communities by forming 400+ Self Help Groups and authoring two books on the subject. Currently,
 he is Managing Trustee of ABSTHA and Director of Rajyavardhini Bank
